Our Multi-Layered Data Sourcing Process

At Data Snap Solutions, we understand that accurate and reliable data is the foundation of successful business growth. That’s why we follow a multi-layered, transparent, and ethical data sourcing process to ensure our clients receive the highest-quality information.

Publicly Available Information

We gather data from trusted public sources such as government filings, trade association directories, industry event registrations, company websites, and business publications. This ensures the data is verifiable and transparent.

Event & Attendee Records

We work closely with industry conferences, trade shows, and exhibitions, where businesses openly share their information for networking and collaboration. Our team collects and verifies attendee lists to help clients connect with decision-makers in their target industries.

Direct Research & Verification

Our in-house research team uses manual verification and AI-powered tools to confirm accuracy. Each contact is validated with the latest professional details, including email addresses, phone numbers, and company information.

Opt-in & Consent-Based Sources

We leverage opt-in partnerships and business networking platforms, ensuring data is collected with consent and aligns with international compliance standards like GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and CCPA.

Data Hygiene & Regular Updates

Our database is continuously updated. We perform:
• Email verification (SMTP & domain checks)
• Phone number validation
• Bounce removal & replacement
• Monthly refresh cycles to ensure active and accurate records.

Customized Targeting

Every business has unique needs. We break down our data by industry, geography, revenue size, employee count, and job title, so clients only pay for the data that aligns with their goals.
